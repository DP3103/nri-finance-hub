NRI Banking & Accounts

NRI Account Types

NRE Account

Rupee-denominated, allows free movement in/out of India

Interest tax-free

NRO Account

For income originating in India like rent/dividends

Interest taxable with TDS

FCNR Account

Fixed deposits in foreign currencies

Interest tax-free during NRI status

RFC Account

For returning NRIs bringing back foreign currency

Taxable after return

GIFT City Account

Foreign currency account in special economic zone

Treated as offshore

RNOR Status Benefits

Qualification

NRI for 9 of last 10 years OR less than 729 days in India in last 7 years

Duration

Up to 3 years after return to India

Key Benefits

  • Foreign income exempt (except India-controlled business)
  • NRE/NRO interest tax-free for 2 years
  • Capital gains concessions
