Three-Bucket Strategy for Retirement

Short-Term Bucket

Purpose: Cover 2-4 years of living expenses

Contents: Cash, savings, short-term debt funds

Medium-Term Bucket

Purpose: Steady inflation-beating income

Contents: Long-term bonds, balanced funds, dividend stocks

Long-Term Bucket

Purpose: Growth for long-term wealth

Contents: Stocks, equity mutual funds, real estate

NRI Banking & Accounts

NRI Account Types

NRE Account

Rupee-denominated, allows free movement in/out of India

Interest tax-free

NRO Account

For income originating in India like rent/dividends

Interest taxable with TDS

FCNR Account

Fixed deposits in foreign currencies

Interest tax-free during NRI status

RFC Account

For returning NRIs bringing back foreign currency

Taxable after return

GIFT City Account

Foreign currency account in special economic zone

Treated as offshore
